About Me

Chenjie Xie (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) received the B.E. degree in information engineering from Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2023. She is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the National Mobile Communications Research Laboratory, Southeast University, Nanjing, China.

Her research interests include channel fingerprints (radio map), massive MIMO communications and intelligent wireless communication.

谢晨洁，本科就读于东南大学吴健雄学院信息工程专业，于2023年获得学士学位，目前于东南大学信息科学与工程学院攻读博士学位（硕博连读）。

当前研究方向主要包括:信道图谱、大规模/超大规模MIMO无线通信、智能通信等。相关研究成果在IEEE JSAC等期刊及IEEE INFOCOM等会议上发表论文数余篇，获授权发明专利数余项。
